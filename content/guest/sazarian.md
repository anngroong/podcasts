__Fr. Serop Azarian__ has served in the Eastern Prelacy of the Armenian Church between 1999-2007, the Western Diocese of the United States between 2007-2010, and the Canadian Diocese 2011-2014. Born in Kuwait, he immigrated to the United States of America in 1987, and served in the US Army as a guardsman and is a Veteran. He pursued his university education in Montclair University, Alliance University and Nazarene Bible College with a Bachelors degree in Divinity and Biblical Studies. Fr. Serop is a priest educator and sporadic visiting pastor since 2014, and presently works in Clark county, Nevada as a court interpreter.
