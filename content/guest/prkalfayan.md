__Dr. Philippe Kalfayan__ is Legal Counsel and lecturer/researcher at the Paris Pantheon Assas Univ./Paris Human Rights Center. He is Assistant Judge HCR at the National Court for Asylum Rights. He served as secretary general of the International Federation for Human Rights (FIDH) and as Legal Expert for the Council of Europe’s Directorate General for Human Rights and Rule of Law. He has authored __La France et l’Imprescriptibilité des Crimes Internationaux__ (Pedone 2015) and __La Réparation des Préjudices en Lien avec les Crimes Historiques__ (TBR Pedone April 2026). He has been involved in institutional reforms and strategic projects in the Republic of Armenian for the last 30 years.
